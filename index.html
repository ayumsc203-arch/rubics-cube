Learning path

Your path from
scrambled to mastery

Follow a structured curriculum built by speedcubing champions. Each milestone unlocks the next, keeping you challenged and motivated.

01
Beginner Foundations
Understand notation, colors, and cube anatomy. Solve your first layer intuitively.
02
First Two Layers (F2L)
Learn to pair and insert corner-edge pairs efficiently into the first two layers.
03
Orient Last Layer (OLL)
Master all 57 OLL algorithms to orient every piece on the top face in one look.
04
Permute Last Layer (PLL)
Execute 21 PLL algorithms to finish the cube in a single efficient last step.
05
Advanced CFOP
Refine look-ahead, full OLL/PLL recognition, advanced F2L, and ergonomic turning.
06
Speedcubing Master
Sub-30, sub-20, sub-10… break your own records and compete on the world stage.
